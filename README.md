# Final-project-web-mining
1. The kind of data you want to find
-   I intend to use the RuneScape Grand Exchange (GE) API and the Real-time Prices API. This includes:

-    Item Metadata: Names, IDs, and members-status of tradeable items.

-    Price Data: High-frequency, real-time "insta-buy" and "insta-sell" prices, along with historical price trends.

-    Volume Data: The quantity of items being traded over specific intervals (e.g., 5-minute or 1-hour blocks).

2. The question/s you want to answer with the data
Using this data, I want to answer the following:

-    Market Volatility: Which high-volume items currently show the largest "spread" (the difference between buying and selling prices) to identify potential "flipping" opportunities?

-    Economic Correlation: Does the price of raw materials (like Runite Ore) show a statistically significant correlation with the price of their finished products (like Rune Bars) over a 24-hour period?(will do other items)

-    Optimal Timing: At what time of day (UTC) is trading volume highest for specific categories of items, such as "Consumables" vs. "Equipment"?
